Five Eyes Warns AI Could Supercharge Cyberattacks Within Months

The Five Eyes intelligence alliance has warned that advanced artificial intelligence (AI) could rapidly reshape the cybersecurity landscape, potentially giving cybercriminals and state-backed hackers far more powerful capabilities within months rather than years. In a joint statement released on Monday, officials from the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand said the pace of AI development is accelerating beyond many industry expectations and could fundamentally change both cyberattacks and cyber defenses.

AI-Powered Cyber Threats Could Arrive Soon

The three-page statement cautioned that increasingly capable AI models may significantly enhance offensive cyber operations, making it easier to identify vulnerabilities, automate attacks, and conduct sophisticated cyber campaigns at greater speed and scale.
